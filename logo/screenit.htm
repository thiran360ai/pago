About Screenit Labs

Screenit addresses one of the notable challenges in the hiring process across the industry.
The platform is disruptive to transform the interview process for organizations to achieve better results in hiring suitable technical skills and provide opportunities for individuals to build a new career as “Interviewer”. Screenit global IT interviewer network is highly professional and resourceful to the global companies. Organizations can outsource technical interviews and the hiring process is made seamless by our dedicated support team.
By creating a marketplace for skilled interviews and solutions to the well-orchestrated interview process, Screenit stands out to be a unique technical assessment platform in this market trend. more
